## Seahawks vs 49ers prediction

**Seahawks vs 49ers Best bet:** Seahawks moneyline (-126)

The 49ers have an explosive attack, but Seattle has a stingy defense that can hold them in check. Seattle ranks fifth in defensive dropback success rate and third in success rate against the run.

Back the more balanced team to take this game and the No. 1 seed in the NFC.
