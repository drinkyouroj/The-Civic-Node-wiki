Microsoft is confronting a new class action lawsuit in San Francisco federal court that accuses the technology company of illegally driving up the cost of generative artificial intelligence services through a secretive agreement with ChatGPT creator OpenAI. According to Reuters, the lawsuit claims Microsoft leveraged its exclusive cloud computing deal with OpenAI to limit the supply of essential computing power needed to operate ChatGPT.
