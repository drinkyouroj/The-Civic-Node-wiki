The story of former President Richard Nixon vs. Arthur Burns, his Federal Reserve chair, is a tale of a president pressuring the head of the central bank for political reasons. Burns fought it, then capitulated, and it laid the foundation for later inflation.

Planet Money's **Kenny Malone** and **Mary Childs** report.

*This segment aired on February 10, 2025.*
