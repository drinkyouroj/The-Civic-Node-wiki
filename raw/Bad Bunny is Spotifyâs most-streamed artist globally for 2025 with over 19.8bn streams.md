This year’s Spotify Wrapped experience introduced several new features for users. The platform added a **“Listening Age” **metric that compares users’ musical preferences with others in their age group based on release years of frequently played tracks. Users can now also view their top albums for the first time, alongside traditional metrics like top songs and genres.

Spotify also incorporated audiobooks into the personalized experience, introducing metrics for top audiobook genres and most-listened titles. **Rebecca Yarros**‘ *Fourth Wing *ranked as the platform’s top audiobook in Premium for 2025.
