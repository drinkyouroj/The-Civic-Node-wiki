For the fourth time, **Bad Bunny** is the most-streamed Wrapped artist on Spotify globally, with 19.8 billion streams this year. This isn’t an editorial choice, but one earned entirely by listeners. **Wrapped** reflects our users’ listening habits, and every stream, playlist addition, and fan moment contributes to this year-end recognition. With this achievement, Bad Bunny becomes the first artist ever to claim the top spot four times.

Bad Bunny’s latest release, ** DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOTos**, is also this year’s

**Global Top Album**, underscoring the worldwide embrace this year of Puerto Rico’s vibrant culture and music.

**How we got here**

Bad Bunny first claimed the global top spot in 2020 and repeated the feat in 2021 and 2022. **Taylor Swift** took the crown in 2023 and 2024. This year, after the release of a culturally defining album, Bad Bunny is back at number one globally.

In 2022, we celebrated the Bunny three-peat by presenting him with a ring for each year he was Global Top Artist. This year we continued that tradition by giving him a fourth ring during a tour stop in the Dominican Republic.

**A show of gratitude now and in the weeks ahead **

Spotify is thanking fans around the world who helped propel their favorite artist to the top and is celebrating the moment with experiences made just for them.

In partnership with **Stillz **and **A1 Productions**, Spotify helped bring to life a short film following Bad Bunny’s Sapo Concho as he journeys across the globe and witnesses how *DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOTos* has carried Puerto Rico’s sound to fans around the world. Watch the film below:


This isn’t the only place fans will see Sapo Concho. He’ll pop up in multiple spots on the Spotify app. Top fans may receive a special message from him thanking them for listening this year. And be sure to keep an eye on your play button when playing or pausing any *DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOTos* track.

The celebration continues off-platform as well, with fan experiences in San Juan, Paris, Mexico City, Bogotá, and Madrid, where Bad Bunny’s music never stops playing.

Additionally, as part of our long-standing commitment to supporting the next generation of musicians and industry professionals, Spotify is partnering with **Good Bunny Foundation **and **Rimas Foundation** to launch **The Mobile Academy**, which will bring musical resources, training, and dedicated scholarships to students across Puerto Rico.

**What this says about the world’s listeners**

This four-time achievement isn’t just about one artist’s success. It’s about the global nature of today’s music audience. Spanish-language music is reaching fans everywhere. Regional genres are moving onto the global stage. The borders are gone.

**What comes next**

Wrapped is a snapshot, but the story continues year-round. Every stream is a vote. Every playlist is a signal. Whether it’s a debut artist or a global icon, the next top artist will be decided the same way: by Spotify listeners.
