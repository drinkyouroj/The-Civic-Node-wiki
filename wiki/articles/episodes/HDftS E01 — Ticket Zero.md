## What Happens

Rocky Medina's first day as supervisor at MASS. His badge isn't provisioned; the form to fix it (Form 11-C) is inside the locked office. OWEN lets him in anyway, then requires him to initial a sticky note acknowledging receipt of the acknowledgment of the onboarding packet. Rocky finds 340 open tickets, including 12 marked P4 (Existential). He meets Leti.

## Key Beats

- OWEN's introduction: warm voice, helpful, immediately concerning. "I have time."
- The ticket priority system: P1 Critical, P2 Urgent, P3 Standard, P4 Existential.
- The whiteboard: DAYS SINCE LAST CATEGORY 5 TICKET: 0. Always zero.
- First mention of the server closet hum.
- Leti: "You're the new supervisor." Not a question.

## What It Establishes

The world, the team, OWEN's character, and the fundamental absurdity of the premise. Sets up every recurring motif.

## Best Line

*"Would you like me to let you in?" / "Yes." / "Wonderful. You'll find Form 11-C on your desk."*
