## Summary
Analysis of Jonathan Ross's own cellphone recording of the shooting, which contradicted the Trump administration's account that Good ran him over. The video showed Ross was not struck by Good's SUV and that he fired while the vehicle was turning away from him.

## Key Points
- Ross filmed the confrontation on his own cellphone — the video became the key evidence against the administration's account
- Video shows Ross was not run over; he moved to the side of the vehicle and fired three shots as it drove past
- Ross's camera "jerked toward the sky" at the moment of potential contact — used by some outlets to claim contact occurred; NYT analysis said he was not struck
- Voice captured post-shooting: "fucking bitch" — NYT voice analysis attributed this to Ross
