## Summary
Profile interview with Becca Good, Renée Good's wife and partner, speaking publicly after Renée's killing by ICE agent Jonathan Ross. Humanizes the victim and provides the family's account of events from Becca's perspective — she was present at the shooting.

- Becca was present during the shooting, recording on her phone; shouted "Drive, baby, drive!" before Good was shot
- After the crash Becca said: "You guys just killed my wife"
- Becca Good was subsequently investigated by DOJ — targeted rather than the shooter
- "Made of sunshine" characterization of Renée from family/friends
