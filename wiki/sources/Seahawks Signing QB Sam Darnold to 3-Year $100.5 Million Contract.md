## Summary

NFL.com report on the Seahawks signing Sam Darnold to a three-year, $100.5 million deal with $55 million guaranteed following his career-best 2024 season with Minnesota (4,319 yards, 35 TDs, 12 INTs under OC Klint Kubiak). Darnold reunited with Kubiak (49ers connection) in Seattle as the centerpiece of the Seahawks' QB-era rebuild under Mike Macdonald.

## Key Points

- 3 years, $100.5M total, $55M guaranteed
- 2024 Vikings stats: 4,319 passing yards, 35 TDs, 12 INTs
- Reunited with OC Klint Kubiak (previously worked together at 49ers)
- Cap hit: $27.5M/year — expensive by normal standards, team-friendly by elite QB standards
- Career arc context: 3rd overall pick 2018 (Jets), Panthers, 49ers (practice squad), Vikings renaissance → Seahawks

## Newsletter Angles

- **Contract structure as cap philosophy**: At $27.5M AAV with $55M guaranteed, Darnold is paid as an elite QB but at discounted risk. The structure reflects the Schneider model — pay fair market for quality, don't overpay for reputation. Compare to Russell Wilson's $35M+ AAV that contributed to his eventual release.
