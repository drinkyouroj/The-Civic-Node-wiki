## Summary

AP wire recap of the NFC Championship — Seahawks 31, Rams 27. Emphasizes Darnold's redemption arc and Macdonald's praise for his QB ("He shut a lot of people up tonight"). Companion to the Yahoo Sports and CBS News versions.

## Key Points

- Macdonald: "You can't talk about the game without talking about our quarterback. He shut a lot of people up tonight, so I'm happy for him."
- Darnold played through an oblique injury — first disclosed in this recap
- JSN on Darnold: "It's been a great first year. We got one more to go. But for him to overcome what he had to overcome, I'm riding with Sam all day."
- Devon Witherspoon on his role in the team: "He's just the heart of our defense. He just is the essence."
- Seahawks' 16-3 overall record entering Super Bowl LX
- Super Bowl LX rematch: first time since 2005 that the same two franchises met in the Super Bowl who had previously played each other
