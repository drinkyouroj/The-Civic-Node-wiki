## Summary
Full transcript of Jack Smith's December 17, 2025 closed-door deposition before the House Judiciary Committee, released December 31. Smith extensively documented Trump's role in the Jan. 6 conspiracy, defended the basis for his investigations, and described the evidence against Trump as strong enough to sustain a conviction.

## Key Points
- Primary source document; full transcript of Smith's testimony
- Smith: Trump "most culpable and most responsible" in conspiracy; riot "does not happen without him"
- Case built substantially on Republican witnesses and allies who cooperated
- Meadows interview placed Jim Jordan in contact with White House during the riot
- Smith defended analyzing Republican lawmakers' phone records as lawful
- Smith rejected characterizations that his investigations were politically motivated
