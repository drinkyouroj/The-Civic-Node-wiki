## Summary

JD Supra legal update with precise vote counts and key information about the Hagerty Amendment that materially changed the GENIUS Act before final passage.

## Key Points

- House vote: 308-122 bipartisan (July 17, 2025).
- Senate vote: 68-30 (June 17, 2025); 17 Democrats joined 51 Republicans.
- House passed the Senate version WITHOUT amendments (no conference needed).
- **Hagerty Amendment**: Senator Bill Hagerty (R-TN) sponsored a substitute amendment that materially changed the GENIUS Act before Senate floor vote — separate from the Banking Committee version. Key changes detailed in full article.
- CLARITY Act: House passed 294-134 on the same day as GENIUS Act (July 17); headed to Senate for fall consideration.
- Signed July 18, 2025; "most significant" Trump crypto regulatory reform to date.
