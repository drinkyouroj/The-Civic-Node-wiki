## Summary

Spotify's 2025 Wrapped announcement confirming Bad Bunny as global top artist for the fourth time, with 19.8 billion streams. His album *Debí Tirar Más Fotos* was named Global Top Album. The announcement framed his achievement as evidence of Spanish-language music's global reach — "the borders are gone."

## Key Points

- 19.8 billion streams in 2025 — global top artist for the 4th time (2020, 2021, 2022, 2025)
- First artist ever to claim the Spotify top spot four times
- *Debí Tirar Más Fotos* won Global Top Album; later won Album of the Year at the 2026 Grammys
- Spotify partnered with Good Bunny Foundation and Rimas Foundation to launch The Mobile Academy — musical resources and scholarships for Puerto Rico students
- Spotify's editorial observation: "Spanish-language music is reaching fans everywhere. Regional genres are moving onto the global stage. The borders are gone."
