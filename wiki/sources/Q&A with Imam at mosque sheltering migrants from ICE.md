## Summary
Interview with the imam of a Minneapolis mosque that was sheltering undocumented immigrants from ICE during Operation Metro Surge. Provides the religious and moral rationale for sanctuary from a Muslim community perspective.

## Key Points
- Minneapolis mosque actively sheltering migrants from ICE during the surge
- Imam articulated religious obligation to provide sanctuary
- Muslim community in Minneapolis (large Somali-American community) was a primary target of the surge despite the fraud rationale
- Sanctuary model extended across faith traditions: Jewish, Muslim, Christian
