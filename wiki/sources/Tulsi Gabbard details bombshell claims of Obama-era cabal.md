## Summary
Fox News reporting on DNI Tulsi Gabbard's claims based on newly declassified documents alleging that Obama-era intelligence officials — Clapper, Brennan, Comey, Rice — manufactured the Russia-Trump collusion narrative to undermine the incoming Trump presidency. Gabbard called it "treasonous" and sent materials to DOJ/FBI for criminal referral. Democrats called the claims "baseless."

## Key Points
- Gabbard released 100+ documents on July 18, 2025 claiming to show Obama directed creation of Russia collusion narrative
- Gabbard's claim: before 2016 election, no evidence Russia tried to alter vote counts; but intelligence community later suggested Putin wanted Trump to win — she argues this shift was politically motivated, not based on new intelligence
- Documents implicate Clapper (DNI), Brennan (CIA), Comey (FBI), Rice (NSA)
- Gabbard sent materials to DOJ and FBI for "criminal referral"; said she expects indictments
- Whistleblowers reportedly coming forward after document release
- Rep. Jim Himes (D-CT, top Dem on House Intelligence): called claims "baseless"
- Obama, Clapper, Brennan, Comey, Rice, Lynch, McCabe: all did not respond to Fox News requests for comment

## Newsletter Angles
- This is the "weaponize the intelligence apparatus for revenge" arc completing: Trump spent his first term claiming he was targeted by a deep state conspiracy; now DNI is releasing documents to make that case officially. Whether the documents prove what Gabbard claims is a different question from the political function this serves
- The criminal referral framing is significant: even if no indictments follow, the referral creates a permanent political narrative that the Obama administration committed "treason"
