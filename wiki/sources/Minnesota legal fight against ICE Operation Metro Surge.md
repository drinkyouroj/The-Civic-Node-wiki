## Summary
Overview of Minnesota's multi-pronged legal strategy against Operation Metro Surge — AG Ellison's suits, cities' suits, federal court orders ICE violated, and the ongoing federal-state legal standoff.

## Key Points
- Minnesota AG, Minneapolis, and St. Paul filed suit against DHS
- Federal courts issued orders ICE repeatedly violated (96+ violations per Judge Schiltz)
- Judge Blackwell found "overwhelming majority" of ICE cases involved people lawfully present
- Legal strategy aimed at both halting the operation and building a record for future accountability
