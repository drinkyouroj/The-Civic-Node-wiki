## Summary
Profile piece on Renée Nicole Good — her background, family, career as a writer and poet, her path to Minneapolis, and her life before being killed by ICE agent Jonathan Ross.

## Key Points
- Good: 37-year-old U.S. citizen; writer and poet; English degree from Old Dominion University
- Had two children from first marriage; one child with second husband (who died in 2023 at 36)
- Originally from Colorado Springs; previously lived in Kansas City; moved to Canada after Trump's 2024 win; then relocated to Minneapolis
- Good's family: did not believe she was an activist; ex-husband said she was on her way home from dropping son at school
- Community described her as "made of sunshine"; "one of the kindest people"
