## Summary
Jim Jordan, chairman of the House Judiciary Committee, demanded that Jack Smith testify before the committee — setting up the December 17, 2025 deposition. Jordan framed the investigation as "weaponization of the DOJ."

## Key Points
- Jordan, as Judiciary Committee chair, demanded Smith's testimony
- Framing: "weaponization" of DOJ against political enemies
- Set up the eventual Dec. 17 closed-door deposition
- Jordan's own contact with the White House on Jan. 6 is documented in the Meadows testimony that came out in the deposition Jordan subsequently forced
