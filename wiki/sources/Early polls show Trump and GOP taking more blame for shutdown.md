## Summary
Polling data from early in the 2025 government shutdown showing Republicans and Trump receiving more blame than Democrats among general public, despite Republican messaging framing Democrats as responsible.

## Key Points
- Public opinion polls: more Americans blamed Trump and Republicans for the shutdown than blamed Democrats
- Pattern consistent with historical shutdown polling; incumbents typically absorb more blame
- Polling context: shutdown entered Day 5+ when these polls fielded

## Newsletter Angles
- Public blame attribution is not purely a function of messaging — it tracks power. The party that holds the White House bears the accountability cost even when they construct a procedural blame-Democrats narrative
