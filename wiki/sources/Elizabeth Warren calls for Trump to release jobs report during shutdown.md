## Summary
Sen. Elizabeth Warren called on Trump to release the monthly jobs report despite the government shutdown, arguing the administration was withholding critical economic data at a moment of market sensitivity.

## Key Points
- Warren accused administration of using the shutdown as cover to suppress economic data
- Jobs report release requires Bureau of Labor Statistics staff — furloughed during shutdown
- Market impact: economic reports delayed during period of tariff uncertainty and shutdown uncertainty
