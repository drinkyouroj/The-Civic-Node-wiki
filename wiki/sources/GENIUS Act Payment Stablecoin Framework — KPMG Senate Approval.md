## Summary

KPMG regulatory insights note on Senate passage of GENIUS Act (June 2025), summarizing the three PPSI categories and key provisions. Written before House passage; notes uncertainty about whether House would take up GENIUS vs. STABLE Act.

## Key Points

- Senate passed GENIUS Act with S.1582 designation.
- Three PPSI categories: IDI subsidiaries, OCC-approved federal qualified issuers, state-qualified issuers.
- STABLE Act (House parallel) not yet decided; unclear which would prevail.
- Broad impact anticipated: banks, retailers, payment systems.
