## Summary

CBS News recap of the NFC Championship Game — Seahawks 31, Rams 27. Companion piece to the Yahoo Sports live-blog. Provides context on the Rams' perspective and the Super Bowl LX setup (Seahawks vs. Patriots, rematch of Super Bowl XLIX from 2015).

## Key Points

- Stafford: 374 yards, 3 TDs; the Rams' offense was elite, undone by a muffed punt by Xavier Smith that directly became a Seahawks TD
- Riq Woolen taunting penalty negated a 3rd-and-12 stop, giving Rams a 1st down → Nacua TD → 31-27
- Witherspoon's pass breakup on 4th-and-4 at the Seattle 6 with 4:59 remaining preserved the win
- Darnold: 346 yards, 3 TDs, 0 turnovers — "first USC Trojan QB ever to start a Super Bowl"
- Seahawks won the season series vs. Rams 2-1 (regular season) plus the playoff game
- Super Bowl LX rematch: Seattle vs. New England (11 years after Super Bowl XLIX — the Malcolm Butler interception game)
