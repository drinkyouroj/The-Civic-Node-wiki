## Overview
Jacob Frey is the mayor of Minneapolis, Minnesota. He became nationally prominent during the George Floyd protests in 2020 and has more recently been a central figure in the legal and political confrontation with the federal government over [[Operation Metro Surge]].

## Key Facts
- Serving as Minneapolis mayor since 2018
- Led the city's legal challenge against federal immigration enforcement operations
- Previously gained national attention during the 2020 policing crisis

## Newsletter Relevance
Frey represents the municipal-level resistance to federal enforcement overreach. His legal strategy against Operation Metro Surge is a test case for how city governments can contest federal power, with implications for federalism and the political dynamics of sanctuary city policies.
