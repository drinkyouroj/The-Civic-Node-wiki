## Overview

Head of FEMA's Office of Response and Recovery (appointed December 2025). Former top health official in Texas. Known for election fraud conspiracy theories amplified by [[Donald Trump]]. Generated headlines for claiming to have teleported to a Waffle House in Rome, Georgia on two occasions. Leads FEMA's largest division with 1,000+ employees and a ~$300 million budget.
