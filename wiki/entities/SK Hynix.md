## Overview

SK Hynix is a South Korean semiconductor company and one of the world's largest producers of DRAM and NAND flash memory. Together with [[Samsung]], it accounts for roughly 70% of global DRAM supply and a major share of high-bandwidth memory (HBM) critical to AI accelerators.

## Key Facts

- Together with [[Samsung]], accounts for ~70% of global DRAM and leading HBM production [[Helium Crisis Tightens Grip On Global Chip Supply Chain]]
- Shares South Korea's ~64.7% helium import dependency on [[Qatar]] [[Helium Crisis Tightens Grip On Global Chip Supply Chain]]
