## Overview

The British Heart Foundation (BHF) is the UK's leading independent funder of cardiovascular research. It funds research into heart and circulatory diseases, operates public health campaigns, and is a major funder of clinical AI research. In this wiki, it appears as the funder of Oxford's AI cardiac diagnostic tool.

## Key Facts

- Funded the University of Oxford AI cardiac diagnostic study (72,000 patients; 86% accuracy; five-year predictive window). [[AI Predicts Heart Disease Five Years Out — Oxford BHF Study]]
- Heart failure remains the second leading cause of death in the UK: 54,000 deaths in 2024, 9.6% of all UK deaths.
- Clinical Director: Dr. Sonya Babu-Narayan, who called early diagnosis "crucial" and the AI tool a potential game-changer for earlier intervention.
