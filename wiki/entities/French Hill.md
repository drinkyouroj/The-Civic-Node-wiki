## Overview

Representative French Hill (R-AR) is the Chairman of the House Committee on Financial Services and the lead House sponsor of the CLARITY Act. He has been the primary architect of crypto market structure legislation in the House across multiple Congress sessions, including FIT21 in the 118th Congress and the CLARITY Act in the 119th Congress.

## Key Facts

- U.S. Representative from Arkansas's 2nd district; House Financial Services Committee Chairman.
- Lead sponsor of the CLARITY Act (H.R. 3633), introduced May 29, 2025.
- Previously led FIT21 (Financial Innovation and Technology for the 21st Century Act) in the 118th Congress.
- CLARITY Act passed House 294-134 on July 17, 2025 — Hill's signature legislative achievement.
- Co-wrote "Crypto Week" op-ed in The Hill and on Agriculture Committee website with Chairman G.T. Thompson.
- Quote: "We are taking historic steps to ensure the United States remains the world's leader in innovation."
- Explicitly framed Biden administration as "openly hostile to crypto and innovation."
- Target for end of September 2025: CLARITY Act passage in the Senate.

## Newsletter Relevance

Hill is the legislative architect of the most sweeping digital asset regulation in U.S. history. The CLARITY Act ending the SEC/CFTC jurisdictional ambiguity is his main achievement. His ability to pass CLARITY 294-134 (bipartisan) shows this has genuine cross-party appeal beyond just Republican crypto maximalism.
