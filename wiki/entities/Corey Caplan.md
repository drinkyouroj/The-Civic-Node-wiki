## Overview

Corey Caplan is the co-founder of [[Dolomite]], a DeFi lending protocol, and simultaneously an adviser to [[World Liberty Financial]], the Trump family crypto venture. This dual role is the insider link in WLFI's controversial borrowing operation.

## Key Facts

- Co-founded [[Dolomite]], the DeFi lending protocol [[Trump's World Liberty Financial uses five billion WLFI to borrow $75 million]]
- Serves as adviser to [[World Liberty Financial]] [[Trump's World Liberty Financial uses five billion WLFI to borrow $75 million]]
- This dual role enabled WLFI to borrow ~$75M against its own tokens on a platform co-founded by its own adviser

## Newsletter Relevance

Caplan is the human link in a textbook [[Conflict-of-Interest Gap]] -- advising the borrower while co-founding the lending platform. In traditional finance, this arrangement would trigger regulatory scrutiny. In DeFi, it operates in a governance vacuum.
