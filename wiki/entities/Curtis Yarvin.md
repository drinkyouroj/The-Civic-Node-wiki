## Overview

Blogger, programmer, and neoreactionary theorist, formerly known by the pseudonym "Mencius Moldbug." Author of the blog *Unqualified Reservations* (2007-2014). Architect of the [[Neoreaction (NRx)]] movement and the concept of [[The Cathedral]] (media/academia/bureaucracy as a progressive meta-structure). Proposes [[Neocameralism]] — governance modeled on corporate structure with a sovereign CEO. His "RAGE" (Retire All Government Employees) plan was adopted by Thiel-backed candidates [[JD Vance]] and [[Blake Masters]] in 2022.
