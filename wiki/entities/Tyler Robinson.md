## Overview
Tyler Robinson (22) is the murder suspect charged in the assassination of Charlie Kirk at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025. He was identified as the shooter and subsequently charged with murder. He surrendered to authorities and reportedly confessed in a Discord group chat approximately two hours before surrendering.

**Note on name:** Early coverage (including some sources indexed in this wiki) mistakenly named the suspect as "Kian Kordestani." Tyler Robinson is the confirmed charged individual per the CNN live blog (September 15, 2025) and FBI Director Kash Patel. The erroneous name has been corrected throughout this wiki.
