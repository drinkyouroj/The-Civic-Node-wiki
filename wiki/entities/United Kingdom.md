## Overview

The UK under PM Keir Starmer is actively positioning itself as an alternative base for US tech companies facing political pressure from the Trump administration. In the Anthropic case, Britain is offering London office expansion and a dual stock listing as incentives to recruit the blacklisted AI company.

## Key Facts

- Department of Science, Innovation and Technology leading Anthropic recruitment effort [[Britain woos Anthropic expansion after US defence clash]]
- Keir Starmer's office backing the effort
- Offers include: London office expansion + dual stock listing
- Explicitly framing this as capitalizing on the US-Anthropic conflict
- Proposals to be presented to Dario Amodei during late May 2026 visit
