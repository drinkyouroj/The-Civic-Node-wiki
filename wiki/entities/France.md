## Overview

Stub. Western European nation-state. Relevant to this wiki through its deliberate light pollution reduction policy — one of the few countries to achieve a significant measurable decrease in artificial light at night through policy intervention.

## Key Facts

- France reduced artificial light at night by 33% through deliberate policy (2014–2022 period), contrasting sharply with the global +16% average [[Light Pollution Brightened Earth 16 Percent Since 2014 — Nature Study]]
