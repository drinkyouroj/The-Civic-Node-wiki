## Overview

Governor of California (since 2019). Democratic politician widely discussed as a potential future presidential candidate. Has emerged as the most prominent institutional counterweight to Trump administration policies at the state level, using California's regulatory and legal infrastructure to push back against federal overreach.

## Key Facts

- Signed SB 664 (2021) establishing a moratorium on new hospice licenses in California due to fraud concerns [[News you won't see on Fox News — California revoked over 280 hospice licenses]]
- Extended the moratorium through AB 177 (2024)
- Established the California Hospice Fraud Task Force — multi-agency body including CDPH, DHCS, DSS, and DOJ's DMFEA
- Filed a civil rights complaint against [[Mehmet Oz]] after Oz publicly attributed hospice fraud to "the Russian Armenian mafia" [[8 arrests in federal crackdown on alleged health care fraud in Southern California]]
- Has framed California's enforcement record as direct rebuttal to federal attacks on state programs
