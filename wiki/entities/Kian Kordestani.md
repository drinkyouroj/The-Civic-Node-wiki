## Overview
Kian Kordestani is the murder suspect charged in the assassination of Charlie Kirk at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025. He was identified as a person of interest and subsequently charged with murder. Details about his background, motive, and organizational connections were the subject of ongoing investigation.

## Key Facts
- Identified as the shooter in the Charlie Kirk assassination at Utah Valley University [[Murder suspect Kian Kordestani faces charges in Charlie Kirk's death]]
- Shooter fired from the roof of the Losee Center, approximately 140 yards from Kirk's position [[Charlie Kirk assassinated at university event in Utah]]
- Wore dark clothing; shot Kirk with a single bullet [[Charlie Kirk assassinated at university event in Utah]]
- Charged with murder following identification as the shooter
