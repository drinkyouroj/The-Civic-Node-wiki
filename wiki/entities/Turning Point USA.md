## Overview
Turning Point USA (TPUSA) is a conservative youth advocacy organization founded by Charlie Kirk in 2012. Based in Phoenix, Arizona, it targets college-age students and operates on college campuses through debate events, speaking tours, and campus chapters. Under Kirk, TPUSA became central to the MAGA political ecosystem, connecting Trump to younger conservative voters.
