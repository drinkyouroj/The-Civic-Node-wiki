## Overview
First and largest cryptocurrency by market cap. Subject of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve, El Salvador's legal-tender experiment, and the maximalist alternative-money thesis.

## Key Facts

- Fixed supply cap: 21 million BTC, hardcoded in the protocol. Fourth halving occurred 2024. [[Satoshi Nakamoto Bitcoin Whitepaper 2008]]
- Created by [[Satoshi Nakamoto]], pseudonymous identity unknown since 2011. Satoshi holds an estimated 1.1M BTC (~$118B at April 2026 prices) that has never moved. [[NYT Names Adam Back as Satoshi Nakamoto — Bitcoin.com Coverage]]
- Crossed $120,000 during U.S. Crypto Week (July 2025). [[Strategic Bitcoin Reserve]]
- Primary investment thesis: [[Bitcoin as Digital Gold]] — a store of value rather than a payment medium.
