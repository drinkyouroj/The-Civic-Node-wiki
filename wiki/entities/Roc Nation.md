## Overview

Roc Nation is an entertainment and sports agency founded by Jay-Z in 2008. It operates across music management, sports representation, and live event production, including producing the NFL's Super Bowl halftime show since 2019.

## Key Facts

- Founded by Jay-Z (Shawn Carter) in 2008
- Entered a partnership with the NFL in 2019 to oversee the league's live music entertainment, including the Super Bowl halftime show
- Manages athletes, musicians, and entertainers across multiple industries
- Produced the Super Bowl LX halftime show featuring [[Bad Bunny]]

Roc Nation's NFL partnership illustrates how cultural production and sports power intersect — the halftime show is one of the most-watched cultural events globally, and the decision of who performs and what they say carries political weight.
