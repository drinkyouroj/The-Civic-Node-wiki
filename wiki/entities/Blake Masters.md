## Overview

2022 Republican Senate candidate for Arizona. Backed by [[Peter Thiel]] with $15 million in primary donations. Adopted [[Curtis Yarvin]]'s [[RAGE (Retire All Government Employees)]] plan as a campaign talking point. Campaign struggled to attract donors beyond Thiel. Part of the pipeline from [[Neoreaction (NRx)]] theory to mainstream Republican politics.
