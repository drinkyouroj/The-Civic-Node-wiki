## Overview

Kickboxer turned social media influencer. Rose to prominence in the [[Manosphere]] during the pandemic era. Accused of rape and human trafficking (denies all criminal wrongdoing). A catalyst for mainstream awareness of the manosphere, particularly after his content reached younger audiences including children as young as 8-10.
