## Overview

British documentary filmmaker known for embedding with extreme or unusual subcultures. Previous subjects include neo-Nazis, Westboro Baptist Church, and Israeli settlers. Released "Inside the Manosphere" on Netflix (March 2026), profiling manosphere influencers. Known for a documentary style that creates safe space for subjects to reveal themselves, then challenges at judicious moments.
