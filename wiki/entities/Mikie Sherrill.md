## Overview

Mikie Sherrill is a Democratic U.S. Representative from New Jersey who won the New Jersey gubernatorial election on November 4, 2025. Her victory was part of the broader Democratic sweep in the first major elections of Trump's second term, alongside [[Abigail Spanberger]] in Virginia and [[Zohran Mamdani]] in New York City.

## Key Facts

- Won the New Jersey gubernatorial election November 4, 2025 [[Election 2025 Historic night for Democrats]]
- One of three Democratic gubernatorial / mayoral wins on the same night, alongside Spanberger (VA) and Mamdani (NYC); voters also approved CA Prop 50 [[Election 2025 key takeaways Democrats score historic big wins]]
- Trump described the night as "not good for Republicans" and blamed his absence from the ballot and the ongoing government shutdown
